This project was my first assignment for my Modeling, Lighting, and Rendering II class, where we went from idea to creation with a 2 week deadline. The elephant was sculpted, UV'd, retopologized, and painted in 3D Coat then brought into Cinema 4D. After finishing up other 3D projects, I would like to come back to this and paint it in Substance Painter, relight the scene, and improve the background.
