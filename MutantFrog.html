This frog has protected this pond for years, and with nuclear waste introduced into it, he has mutated to grow horns and a second pair of eyes. This project was done in 2 weeks for class, and constructed in 3D Coat and Cinema 4D. I will be going back and re-doing this scene eventually. If you cover one set of eyes, the frog transforms from bullfrog to mutant.
